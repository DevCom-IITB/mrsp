Rules and Regulations

Please go through all the rules and regulations pertaining to the residence allocation system before continuing to registration.

  • Horizontal transfer request of quarters from Tulsi to MRSB or viceversa is not applicable from 01st July 2021. The above rule does not apply for Type-1 quarter. They are allowed one transfer to either Tulsi or MRSB based on the application seniority.
  • While applying for married quarter one can opt for any of the above quarters or all the above with choice preference. Allocation will be done based on the application seniority and not based on registration. The application seniority will be maintained even if the lower choice accommodation is offered, however, not accepted.
  • It is the responsibilty of the resident to vacate the allotted quarter within the allowed period of stay. Any overstay without permission from A.Dean.SA hereafter will attract a fine of Rs. 500/- perday and if requried the security will be authorised to lock the quarter, if overstay without any intimation is noted.
  • Damage/modification or drawing on the interior or outer walls of the alloted quarter will attract heavy fines and if required eviction of the quarter and cancellation of the application request.
  • Need to pay Rs. 5000/- as deposit for married quarter accommodation.
  • It is the duty of vacating resident to clean the flat before handover. Any disposable materials should be disposed.
  • No material/items/furnitures should be kept outside the flat in the veranda/lobby or in the quarter premise. If found will be taken without intimation and will be disposed as scrap. Unclaimed cycles/two-wheelers will be disposed as scrap.
  • Residents are allowed to keep only 01 two-wheeler and 01 four wheeler or 02 two wheeler. The vehicles should bear proper IIT registration. Any vehicles without IITB stickers will attract fine as appropriate.
  • It is expected that the residents need to follow the protocols of fair behaviour and avoid any nuisances to the neighbour. Loud musics or anyother activities that case disturbances to other will lead to appropriate actions.
  • Cigarrete smoking, alcohol and banned substances are strictly prohibitted in the academic campus.
  • For any functions in the quarter premises obtain permission from HCU office. It is also the duty of the residents to keep the quarter, lobby, lifts, stairs and surrounding clean. Violation will lead to fine for the entire residents.
